Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom jewelry sizing?

Yes! We provide professional resizing for rings and custom lengths for necklaces and bracelets to ensure your piece fits perfectly.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Accra takes 1-2 business days. For custom orders or locations outside Accra, please allow 3-5 business days.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality materials including 18k gold plating, sterling silver, and authentic gemstones. Detailed material info is available on each product page.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order is processed, we will send you a tracking update via WhatsApp or Email so you can monitor its journey.
